Introducing SDL EduPro: Streamlining School Administration with Technology

SDL EduPro is the perfect solution for administrators looking to streamline their school’s administrative processes. The software’s intuitive and user-friendly interface makes it easy to manage and track student information, assignments, and report cards. With EduPro, teachers, administrators, and students can all enjoy the benefits of digitalised school administration.

The H2R software is used by HR professionals to manage the employee details from hire to retirement. The HR department can sync data from multiple systems and keep track of their employees, save hours of time in processing data/forms by completing easy tasks for managers and employees alike.
